FAQ

How do I create a trip?

Tap "+" on the home screen, enter the details, and share the code with friends.

How do I add expenses?

Open the trip -> Add Expense -> enter the amount -> choose who paid and how to split it.

How do I settle up?

Check the balances tab and mark payments as settled once you receive them.
