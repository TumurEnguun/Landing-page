Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 14, 2025

1) Who we are

Mandarin ("we", "us", "our") provides an expense-sharing app.

Contact: support@mandarin.app

2) What we collect & why

  • Account info: name, email, password hash or social login ID. Purpose: account creation/login.
  • Trip & expense data: titles, participant names, expenses, and splits. Purpose: balances and reimbursements.
  • Device & logs: IP address, device info, crash logs. Purpose: security and debugging.

We do not collect payment card or bank details.

We do not sell data or show ads.

We share data only with service providers (hosting, database, email) and authorities if required. Providers act under our instructions.

We use TLS, access controls, backups, and other safeguards appropriate to risk.

5) Your rights

Request access, correction, or deletion via support@mandarin.app or use Delete Account in the app.
